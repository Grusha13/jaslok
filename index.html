Jaslok Hospital and Research Centre is one of the oldest tertiary care, multi-specialty hospitals in Mumbai, India. It was established in 1973 and has since become a prominent name in healthcare across the country. Located on Pedder Road in South Mumbai, the hospital offers a wide range of medical services, including general surgery, cardiology, oncology, neurology, orthopedics, and many others.

Jaslok Hospital is known for its advanced medical facilities, highly skilled doctors, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. It has a strong focus on both patient care and medical research, often participating in various clinical trials and studies to advance medical science. The hospital is equipped with modern technology and provides specialized treatment options, including organ transplants, robotic surgeries, and advanced imaging services.
